The "Visa Sprint":
How to Ensure You Make it to 2026

By FIFA La Vida Travel Advisor

The biggest opponent for international fans at the 2026 World Cup won't be the heat or ticket prices—it will be the bureaucracy.

With the tournament spread across the United States, Canada, and Mexico, logistics are more complex than ever. But the most critical bottleneck is the US B1/B2 Visitor Visa. Wait times in key football nations like Mexico, Colombia, and India are currently stretching into 2027—past the tournament finals.

If you are planning to travel, you cannot afford to wait. Here is the expert-backed "Apply & Accelerate" strategy to secure your entry.

The "Apply & Accelerate" Strategy

Don't Wait for Tickets to Go on Sale

Follow this specific timeline to beat the queue:

1
Apply NOW (Pre-Ticket) Submit your DS-160 and pay the fee. Accept any appointment date, even if it's in 2027. You need to be in the system.
2
Buy Your Ticket (Late 2025) When FIFA opens sales, secure your match ticket. Save the official confirmation email immediately.
3
Request "Expedited" Appointment Log back into the visa portal. Select "Emergency/Expedited" appointment. Upload your ticket as proof of a "planned event."

Phase 1: Planning Your Route

Before applying, you need a rough itinerary. This tournament is unique because it spans an entire continent. A match in Seattle followed by a match in Monterrey isn't just a flight; it's an international border crossing.

Phase 3: The "Ticket Holder" Shortcut

How it Works

The US State Department is aware of major sporting events. Historically, for events like the Olympics or World Cups held on US soil, embassies implement ad-hoc policies to prioritize attendees.

CRITICAL: This is a discretionary tool used by Consular chiefs. It is not a guarantee. This is why having your application already in the system is vital. If you wait until 2026 to apply, the "Expedite" slots might already be full.

Summary Checklist

  • Check your passport expiration (must be valid 6 months beyond July 2026).
  • Apply for the US Visa NOW to lock in a slot.
  • Research venues at fifalavida.com/#venues to guess where your team might play.
  • Subscribe to official embassy newsletters in your country for announcements regarding "Special Event Appointments."
