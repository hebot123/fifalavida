The Wild West of Tickets:
Safe Buying Guide 2026

By FIFA La Vida Market Analyst

If you are confused by the dozen websites claiming to sell World Cup tickets right now, you aren't alone. With the draw completed, the scammers are out in full force.

We have broken down the only legitimate places to buy/sell tickets (and the "grey market" sites you should avoid) to help you keep your money safe.

Tier 1: The "Official" Ecosystem (Safe)

These are the only platforms sanctioned by FIFA. If you buy here, your entry is 100% guaranteed.

A. Official FIFA Resale

Status: Not Open Yet

The only place to buy actual tickets (not tokens) officially with zero fraud risk.

  • Pros: 100% Guaranteed. Instant transfer.
  • Cons: Face Value only (usually).
  • Fees: ~15% for Buyers.

B. FIFA Collect (Tokens)

Status: Open Now

Marketplace for "Right to Ticket" tokens on Polygon. You buy the guarantee to a seat.

  • Pros: Secure specific teams (e.g., Brazil) now.
  • Cons: High complexity (Crypto/Wallets).
  • Risk: Must "convert" token in May 2026.

Tier 2: The "Grey Market" Giants

StubHub, Viagogo, VividSeats

These sites are open now, but they come with significant risks and massive fees.

!
The "Speculative" Trap Most sellers here do NOT have tickets yet. They are betting they can win one later to send to you. If prices spike, they may cancel your order.
$
Massive Fees Expect to pay 25% - 35% in service fees at checkout. A $500 ticket quickly becomes $675.

Tier 3: The "Wild West" (Extreme Caution)

Social media marketplaces like Reddit, Facebook, and X/Twitter.

CRITICAL WARNING: Since "Ticket Transfers" are not fully open/easy yet, nearly everyone selling on social media without proof is a risk.

Rule #1

Never pay via "Friends & Family" (Zelle, CashApp). If they refuse PayPal Goods & Services, it is a scam.

Rule #2

Screenshots are fake. Ask for a screen recording of them logging into the FIFA app and navigating to the ticket.

Rule #3

Static PDF tickets do not exist for this tournament. If someone emails you a PDF, it is 100% fake.

Summary Recommendation

  • Safest Choice: Wait for the Official FIFA Resale Platform (Spring 2026).
  • Smartest "Early" Choice: Buy "Right to Ticket" tokens on FIFA Collect if you understand crypto.
  • Last Resort: StubHub/Viagogo, but be prepared for high fees and potential cancellation risks.
